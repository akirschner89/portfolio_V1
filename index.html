About me

I am a musician turned developer based in Austin, TX. I love to build new, useful apps and learn about current technologies. While I've always been interested in web and software development, I am excited to pivot to full-time development.

I was first exposed to web development while working in sales for a SaaS startup for paid social advertising. While I experienced success in this role, I regretted that I was unable to incorporate the ingenuity I engaged in as a musician into this position. This led me to the Coding Bootcamp program at the University of Texas, Austin, where I discovered the world of web development and found that it fulfilled the need to utilize both innovative technologies with creative conceptualizations.
