About me

I'm a musician turned developer Based in Austin, TX. I love to build new, useful apps and learn about new technologies. Web and software development have always been an interest; I'm extremely excited to make the pivot from a hobby to a full-time career. The sky's the limit!

My first exposure to web development was while working in sales for a SaaS startup for paid social advertising. Although I experienced success in this role, there was a sense of fulfillment missing. I decided to begin exploring the world of web development and it was akin to pandora's box opening. All of the sudden, I sensed a strong grasp on the subject and have been excited ever since to learn about all of the technologies available - to build useful, interesting, and exciting applications.
